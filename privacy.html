PulseStrum

PulseStrum ("the app") is developed and operated by Tapirus Studio. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

PulseStrum does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login is required.
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting is sent to external servers.
  • No advertising identifiers are accessed.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your saved rhythm patterns and settings locally on your device using the device's standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

In-App Purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases processed by Apple App Store and Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information. Please refer to Apple's and Google's privacy policies for details on how purchase transactions are handled.

Children's Privacy

PulseStrum does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no features that require personal data from any user of any age.

Contact

Questions? Email tapirus.studio@gmail.com.

fut.io

fut.io is a browser-based training tool. It runs entirely in your browser and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Any settings you configure (timers, intervals) exist only in your browser session.

ADA Scanner

ADA Scanner is a command-line tool distributed via GitHub, not a hosted service. It does not collect or transmit any personal information about the people who run it. When you use it to scan a website, it processes the URL(s) you supply and any publicly accessible page content from those sites in order to generate an accessibility report — that data is processed locally/in your own CI environment and is not sent to Tapirus Studio.

Local AI Setup

Local AI Setup is a collection of configuration scripts distributed via GitHub, not a hosted service. It does not collect or transmit any personal information. All configuration and model activity happens locally on your own machine.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
