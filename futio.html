fut.io — FUT Randomizer

Cognitive-load training for soccer players.

Soccer TrainingWeb Tool

fut.io is a free browser-based training tool built to help soccer players practice scanning and decision-making under visual and cognitive load. Inspired by scan-training methods used in professional academies, it was adapted for independent coaching use.

What It Does

The tool displays randomized numbers or simple equations on screen, and randomizes background colors and shapes on a configurable timer — forcing players to keep scanning and reacting rather than settling into a predictable rhythm. A "Blink" mode adds flashing intervals for an extra layer of visual stimulus, and a wake-lock keeps the screen active during a training session so it won't dim mid-drill.
