PulseStrum Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

PulseStrum ("the app") is developed and operated by Tapirus Studio. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

PulseStrum does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login is required.
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting is sent to external servers.
  • No advertising identifiers are accessed.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your saved rhythm patterns and settings locally on your device using the device's standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

In-App Purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases processed by Apple App Store and Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information. Please refer to Apple's and Google's privacy policies for details on how purchase transactions are handled.

Children's Privacy

PulseStrum does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no features that require personal data from any user of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
